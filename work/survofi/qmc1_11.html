In types PERMUTATIONS and SUBSETS the default elements are 1,2,3,...
These elements can be replaced by other symbols by a specification
SYMBOLS=<list_of_symbols>.
Example:
COMB P,CUR+1 / P=SUBSETS,5,3 SYMBOLS=a,b,c,d,e
Subsets of size 3 of 5 elements: N[P]=10
