Encapsulated PostScript files made by other systems can be included in
the Survo PostScript printing by inserting a control line of the
form
  - epsfile <EPSfile>,x,y
in the PRINT list. The options are the same as for the - picture
lines (see PSPICT?)

For the plots made by Survo itself,
  - picture <PSfile>,x,y
given in PLOT by DEVICE=PS,<PSfile> should always be preferred.
