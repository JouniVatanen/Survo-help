When making Flash demos, activations and mouse clicks can be
recorded by inserting a line

act_sounds=SUR_ACT,SUR_ML,SUR_MR

in the SURVO.APU file. The default WAV files listed above are
in the directory <Survo>\U\SND.
After making this modification and restarting Survo, the sounds
are activated by keystrokes F2 L E and deactivated by F2 L e.
When the sounds are activated,
the first sound will heard when a command is activated by ESC or
a double-click.
The second sound relates to the mouse click by the leftmost button.
The third sound relates to the mouse click by the rightmost button.
