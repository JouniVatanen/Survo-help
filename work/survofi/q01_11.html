Links and cross references in Survo

Survo offers many means for navigation within Survo applications as
well as for linking to other programs and to the web.
In fact any word or sequence of words typed in the edit field
may be used as a link to the Web.

  1 = Menus for job control and lists of references 
  2 = Desktop operations (by Kimmo Vehkalahti) 
  3 = Inquiry system (HELP) 
  4 = Navigation in the edit field 
  5 = Soft keys or buttons 
  6 = Links to other programs and to the web 
  7 = F1 codes (links) for getting information from the Web (WEB?)
