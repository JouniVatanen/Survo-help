COMB L,CUR+1 / L=LATTICE,n MIN=MIN1,MIN2,...,MINn MAX=MAX1,MAX2,...,MAXn
lists all points in an n-dimensional rectangular lattice where
the integer-valued coordinates Xi (i=1,2,...,n) are limited by MINi<=Xi<=MAXi.
Example:
COMB L,CUR+1 / L=LATTICE,3 MIN=0,1,2 MAX=1,2,4
Lattice points in 3 dimensions: N[L]=12
0 1 2
0 1 3
0 1 4
0 2 2
0 2 3
0 2 4
1 1 2
1 1 3
1 1 4
1 2 2
1 2 3
1 2 4

    C = Other forms of COMB
