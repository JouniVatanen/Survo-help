COMB <name>,L
with a specification <name>=<type>,<list_of_parameters> 
lists various combinatorial entities like
permutations, subgroups, partitions, distributions, etc.
in the edit field from line L onwards.
COMB <name> TO <text_file> 
does the same job but saves the results in a text file.

Example:
COMB S1,CUR+1 / S1=SUBSETS,10,5 SYMBOLS=A,B,C,D,E,F,G,H,I,J
Subsets of size 5 of 10 elements: N[S1]=252
A B C D E
A B C D F
A B C D G
A B C D H
. . . . .
E G H I J
F G H I J

   C = Various forms of COMB 
   M = More on mathematical operations
