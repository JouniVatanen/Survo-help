Estimating transition probabilities from a given sample

MARKOV L1,L2 / MATRIX=<matrix> STATES=<list_of_states> ORDER=<integer> 
estimates <matrix> of transition probabilities on the basis of a sample
sequence of one-character state names on edit lines L1-L2.
The STATES specification tells the list of the state names.

An example of this operation is found in the tutorial
/MARKOV-DEMO2
