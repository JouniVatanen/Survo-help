In pie charts any of the sectors may be pulled out by adding P after
the shading value. The distance of the new location of the sector will
be 20 per cent of the radius. To alter this distance, use P3 instead
of P for 30 per cent distance, for example.
If SHADING is not given, an ascending set of shading values is used
with regular intervals.
