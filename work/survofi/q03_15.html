Calling other programs from Survo

Other programs may be started during a Survo session by a command of
the form
> <pathname_of_program_file> 
Due to '> ' in front of the command the Survo session may be continued
normally and in a similar way several other programs may be put into
action simultaneously.

For example, another Survo session is called by
> <Survo>\U\S.EXE
This command may have a parameter, a name of a sucro that starts the
other Survo session.
The command
><pathname_of_program_file> 
(only one '>' in front the command) does the same thing but the Survo
session will be frozen until the other program has been terminated.
