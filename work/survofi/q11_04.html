Extra specifications for bar/pie charts:

  1 = HOME, SIZE, XDIV, YDIV, PEN, LINETYPE 
  2 = SCALE 
  3 = FRAME, GRID, TICK, TICKLENGTH (boxes and grids) 
  4 = HEADER 
  5 = TEXTS (additional labels and texts) 
  6 = SHADING, LEGEND 
  7 = VALUES, LABELS, NAMES (writing text in bars/pies) 
  8 = GAP 
  9 = PLAN (in pie charts) 
  0 = MINVALUE 
  A = ANGLE (in pie charts) 
  M = MAX (in pie charts) 
  G = GROUPING (bar chart of several groups) 
  L = LAB_DELIMITER (in horizontal bar grphs) 
  P = More information on plotting in general
