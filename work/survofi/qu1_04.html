Goodness of fit test for an n x 2 table

TABTEST <table>,L / FIX=FIT
computes the Pearson goodness of fit test for an n x 2 table of
integers. In the first column the observed frequencies
and in the second column integers proportional to the expected
frequencies are given.
After the common Chi-squared approximation for P is obtained
the P value is also computed by simulation according to the same
principle as in other forms of TABTEST.
