Sucros made in SURVO 84C and 98 work similarly in SURVO MM.
In fact, the regular collections of sucros located in the S and TUT
When a sucro is activated in SURVO MM, it is searched for in this order:

  1. Current data directory of SURVO MM (for example, D:\DEMO1)
  2. `sucropath' defined in SURVO.APU of SURVO MM
  3. <Survo>\U\S   (<Survo> is the main directory of SURVO MM.)
  4. <Survo>\U\TUT (This directory is not needed anymore.)
  5. <Survo>\S
  6. <Survo>\TUT

By the sucro command
/WHICH <name_of_sucro> 
the current instance of a sucro is automatically detected.
