PLAY is a Survo operation for creating of sound files (WAV)
and for playback of them

  1 = PLAY SOUND(S) for playback of WAV files 
  2 = PLAY DATA     for creating sound files from Survo data 
  3 = Sound signals in tutorials 
  4 = Sound signals for mouse clicks and activations
