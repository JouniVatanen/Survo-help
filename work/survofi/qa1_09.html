/MTEST-COV2       / Equality of several covariance matrices
Comparing several covariance matrices of  multinormal samples
by an asymptotic test.
This sucro must be activated in the form
/MTEST-COV2
without parameters but with specifications
COV=COV1,COV2,COV3,...
SIZES=N1,N2,N3,...
where COV gives the names of the covariance matrices and SIZES gives
the sample sizes
Each covarince matrix is obtained usually by activating the /COV sucro
after the CORR operation.
