FILLS=<list_of_fill_specifications> 
gives a list of fill definitions for the current graph. Each member
of this list is a word (name of the fill) and any such a definition
must be given in the form

   <name_of_the_fill>=<x>,<y>,<fill_index> 

where <x>,<y> are the coordinates (in plotting units) of the point in-
side the area to be filled and <fill_index> gives the fill type. FILLS is
not valid in PostScript plotting.
