MAT M=#MAGIC(m)
creates an m*m magic square matrix M of integers 1,2,...,m^2 where all column
and row sums as well as the diagonal and skew diagonal sums are the same
i.e. equal to m*(m^2+1)/2. The dimension m must be greater than 2.
