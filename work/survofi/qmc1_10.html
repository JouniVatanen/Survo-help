In all forms of COMB only the number of combinations is displayed when
RESULTS=0 is given.
Example:
COMB P1,CUR+1 / P1=PARTITIONS,70,14  RESULTS=0
Partitions 14 of 70: N[P1]=290071
................................................................................
COMB P2,CUR+1 / P2=PARTITIONS,70 MAX=14 RESULTS=0
Partitions of 70: N[P2]=1614987
................................................................................
COMB P3,CUR+1 / P3=PARTITIONS,70 MAX=13 RESULTS=0
Partitions of 70: N[P3]=1324916
Note: difference N[P2]-N[P3]=290071  = N(P1)  (Why?)
................................................................................
By giving RESULTS=1 the list will be displayed but the first line
(header line containing N[P]) not.

    C = Other forms of COMB
