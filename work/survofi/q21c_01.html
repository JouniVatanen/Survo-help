Central limit theorem

Certain sucros are available for demonstrating the importance
of the central limit theorem.
This theorem says that - under very general conditions - the sum
of independent random variables tends to the normal distribution
when the number of summands grows.

Demonstration programs:
/DISTRSUM (In English)
/SATSUMMA (In Finnish)

Se also:
   1 = TRANSFORM <data> BY DISTR(P) (|EXAMPLE| in Finnish) 
   D = Probability distributions
