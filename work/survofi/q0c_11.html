Mouse: Moving the cursor and activating commands:

Traditionally in Survo the cursor is moved in the edit field window
by the arrow keys, etc. and commands touched by the cursor are activated
by the ESC key,
In SURVO MM it is usually faster to move the cursor by the mouse by
clicking the sought position. A double-click (by the left button)
activates a Survo command if such a one is indicated by the mouse.

A double-click by the right button starts the help system of Survo
and information will be shown about the keyword indicated by the
mouse. Please note that, in principle, any word in the edit field
is a potential link to the help system.
