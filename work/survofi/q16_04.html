Extra specifications in curve plotting:

  1 = HOME, SIZE, XDIV, YDIV, PEN, LINETYPE 
  2 = XSCALE, YSCALE (scales) 
  3 = FRAME, GRID, TICK, TICKLENGTH, TICKTURN (boxes and grids) 
  4 = HEADER, XLABEL, YLABEL (labels) 
  5 = TEXTS (additional labels and texts) 
  6 = FILL, YFILL, OFILL, IFILL 
  7 = INTEGRAL 
  8 = COLOR_CHANGE (individual color for each curve in GPLOT) 
  9 = AXES (moving the coordinate axes) 

  P = more information on plotting in general
