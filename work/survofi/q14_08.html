FRAME=<0,1,2,3,4,5 or 6> 
(in PLOT operation) controls plotting of frames around the graph.
FRAME=2 is default and means that all frames are plotted.
FRAME=1 erases the outer frame.
FRAME=0 erases all frames and coordinate axes notations.
FRAME=3 erases the inner frame and coordinate axes notations.
FRAME=4 erases the inner frame.
FRAME=5 erases the top and rightmost line in the inner frame.
FRAME=6 as 5 but erases also the outer frame.

PLOT /FRAME
is a special operation for frame plotting according to HOME, SIZE,
HEADER, FRAME, PEN, FRAMES, TEXTS, FILLS and LINES specifications.
(See PLOTFR?)
